About the data

The charts use seven-day rolling averages of newly reported cases per 100,000 residents. Rates allow states with different population sizes to be compared, while rolling averages reduce day-to-day reporting noise.

The data represents reported cases rather than every infection. Testing availability, reporting practices, and retrospective revisions changed during the pandemic.

Data: The New York Times COVID-19 Data Repository . Geographic boundaries: us-atlas , derived from U.S. Census Bureau cartographic boundaries.
